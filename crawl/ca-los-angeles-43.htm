The Los Angeles Dog Kennel Alternative

Home to the poshest of pooches. That's why dog boarding in Los Angeles is becoming increasingly popular. The energized LA dog can be your Doga partner (Dog + Yoga = Doga), your co-worker in pet-friendly offices, and most of all, your best friend. In Los Angeles, fabulous furry friends are no stranger to the big screen, as Hollywood has had its fair share of celebrity dogs (Lassie, anyone?). Los Angeles dog lovers tend to live hectic lives, so it's no surprise when the time comes for the hard working Angeleno to take a business trip or two.
