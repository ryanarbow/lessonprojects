The Houston Dog Kennel Alternative

Many dogs reside in Houston; after all, it is America's fourth largest city. Dog parents in the area stroll with their furkids through the city's wonderful parks like Tranquility, Sam Houston, and Memorial. While dogs are not allowed in the Johnson Space Center or the Galleria, they are allowed in local hotspots like the Houston Arboretum and Nature Center. Yep, the Houston dog parent has it good. Especially if they need to leave town.


Houston dog boarding hosts are some of the best in the country. They say everything's bigger in Texas, and DogVacay.com's professional pet sitting hosts and dog lovers have some of the biggest play areas and yards of any group in the entire country. If your pup needs more excitement, your Houston dog boarding host might bring her to Boneyard Dog Park & Drinkery or Bill Archer Dog Park for some tail wagging fun. With pet services including home dog boarding, dog walking, dog grooming, doggy daycare, and pet sitting, DogVacay.com's Houston dog boarding hosts are sure to give your pooch a vacation to remember!
