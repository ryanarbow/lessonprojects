The San Diego Dog Kennel Alternative

From dog-friendly hot spots like Rancho Bernardo Park and Ocean Beach to enticing local, pet-friendly attractions and activities, America's Finest City is the ideal city to live in with your canine companion. It's no wonder that San Diego is home of PETCO Park and numerous pet-friendly restaurants within the Downtown, East Village and Gaslamp Quarter. Dog boarding in San Diego is ideal with so many outdoor activities at the San Diegoan's disposal, furry friends and beach buddies are welcome with open arms and paws.

It's no wonder that San Diego's pet sitting community and home dog boarding options have become the preferred option for traveling dog owners.
