The Boston Dog Kennel Alternative

Whether you live in Back Bay or Castle Island, Boston is home to a plethora of dog parks and pet-friendly neighborhoods. With its rich history and scenic views, dog boarding in Boston is most notably known for catering to canine needs no matter the breed. From cruising around the Boston Harbor Islands with their four-legged best friend to taking a road trip to Cape Cod, Bostonians know how to show their pups some love. But not all travel destinations are dog-friendly, which is why DogVacay.com has a list of trusted and insured Boston dog boarding hosts who offer dog walking, doggy daycare, and home dog boarding.


Boston provides endless opportunities for furry-friend fun and DogVacay.com's Boston pet sitting hosts know just the right hot spots. If your canine companion needs to go for a fun filled dog walk, the Charlestown Navy Yard's 30-acre lot will not disappoint and your dog boarding host is happy to accommodate. The South End of Boston also boasts numerous dog bakeries, dog boutiques, and 13 off-leash dog parks including Peter's Park and Joe Wex Dog Park. Enough entertainment to ensure that your Boston pet sitting host will spoil your pooch and she'll be returned to you tired and happy! DogVacay's Boston dog boarding hosts are ready to give your pooch a vacation like none other, so pack your dog leash and get your little one ready for some tail waggin' fun!
