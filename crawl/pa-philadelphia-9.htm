The Philadelphia Dog Kennel Alternative

Looking for trusted and insured home dog boarding and pet sitting in Philadelphia? You're in luck! DogVacay.com's Philadelphia dog boarding hosts are ready to give your pooch a vacation to remember. Home to PAWS, Philadelphia's largest rescue organization, the City of Brotherly Love has pet sitting professionals and dog lovers roaming South Street with a Philly cheese steak in one hand and a dog leash in the other. Philly's dog-friendly vibe roars louder than the Liberty Bell, with local pet-friendly attractions gracing the city's streets.


Philadelphia dog boarding is the best option for the traveling doggie parent, and DogVacay.com is the most efficient service that connects trusted dog boarding hosts with doggy parents. The fun never stops, as Philadelphia dog boarding hosts can take guest pooches to multiple dog-friendly locations. The Piazza at Schmidts, an outdoor dog-friendly plaza, is the ideal hub to shake paws with new canine companions while Schuylkill River Dog Park, Passyunk Square Dog Park or the Mario Lanza Park are a must for the pup who needs to work off a lot of rambunctious energy. No matter the activity, DogVacay's Philadelphia pet sitting hosts are pro's in finding the hottest hot spots in The City that Loves You Back.
