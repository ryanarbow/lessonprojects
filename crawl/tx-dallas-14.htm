The Dallas Dog Kennel Alternative

Dallas is home to sports events, must-see monuments, and, of course, Dallas dogs. If your Dallas pet sitting host isn't playing fetch or taking in the local dog parks with your pup, you might find them enjoying a meal at Bolsa in Oak Cliff, Southpaw's Organic Caf? in University Park or Taverna Pizzeria And Risottoria in Uptown.
