The Seattle Dog Kennel Alternative

With amazing attractions such as the renowned Space Needle and the bustling Pike Place market, Seattle Center and Pioneer Square, there's never a dull moment in Seattle, WA. With a number of dog-friendly neighborhoods surrounding the city, like Greenlake, Ballard, Magnolia, and Fremont, Seattle pooches have no shortage of pet-friendly parks, restaurants, and Starbucks. Needless it say, it's great to be a dog in the Emerald City. But when rainy Seattle drives Seattleites to take a short sunny escape, it's no wonder they turn to DogVacay.com's trusted and insured Seattle dog boarding hosts when they travel. DogVacay is the best service that connects traveling dog owners with Seattle pet sitting professionals and dog lovers who provide pet services such as dog walking, doggy daycare, and home dog boarding.


Want your furry friend to have some outdoor fun while you're away? DogVacay's Seattle dog boarding hosts are ready to take your pup to local pet-friendly spots such as Magnuson Park, a doggy playground paradise, to meet some other furbabies and have fun while doing so. Whether your Seattle pet sitting host is taking Spot on a dog walk or grooming him for social outings, you can travel with the peace of mind that your best friend is in good hands!
